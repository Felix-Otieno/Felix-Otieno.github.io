TOURISM PLACES

Nairobi Mamba Village

The home of Crocodiles in Kenya's capital city.

Ngong Hills

A short drive from the city, the hills are home to a wide variety of wildlife.

Snake Park

some impressive snake species, including the puff adder, black mamba, Egyptian cobra, African rock python and Gaboon viper.

Giraffe Center

Take the nature trail to the Gogo River and search for giraffes in the wild.

Uhuru Gardens

Pretty gardens and interesting. Have good views of Nairobi's skyline.
